Our Specialities


Laboratory Services

Laboratory services in a hospital are crucial for diagnosing and managing various medical conditions. They involve testing samples taken from patients to help diagnose diseases, monitor health conditions, and guide treatment.

  • Clinical Chemistry
  • Hematology

Outpatient

Outpatient services provide medical care without requiring an overnight stay. They include routine consultations with doctors, diagnostic tests like blood work and imaging, minor surgical procedures.

  • Therapies
  • Routine Consultations

Emergency

Emergency services offer immediate care for acute and life-threatening conditions. They include treatment in the (ED) for emergencies like trauma, heart attacks, and strokes. as well as critical care for severe cases.

  • Emergency Surgery
  • Critical Care
